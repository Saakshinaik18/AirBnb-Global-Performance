Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard🏠

📊Project Description:
Developed an interactive Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard using Power BI to analyze listing growth, market share, pricing trends, customer reviews, and host trust metrics across multiple cities. The dashboard provides insights into 279K+ listings across 10 major cities and 182K hosts, helping understand market performance, seasonal demand patterns, and customer behavior. Interactive visualizations allow stakeholders to evaluate city-level market share, review frequency, property type pricing, and listing lifecycle trends.

🎯 Project Objectives:
Analyze Airbnb market share across major cities
Understand property pricing trends by room type
Evaluate review frequency and customer engagement
Identify top-performing cities and hosts

💼Business Impact:
The dashboard enables data-driven decision making for Airbnb stakeholders by:
Identifying high-performing cities such as Paris, NYC, and Sydney contributing nearly half of total listings and 48% of reviews.
Highlighting pricing differences across property types, showing hotel rooms priced significantly higher than private or shared rooms.
Analyzing review frequency and trust indicators, helping understand customer engagement and host credibility.
Detecting seasonal demand patterns across cities, allowing better pricing and listing strategies.
Providing insights into listing lifecycle trends, including growth phases and the impact of market regulations and COVID-19.

📈Key Results / Insights:
Paris recorded the highest number of listings and reviews, indicating strong market demand.
Mexico City and Rio achieved the highest customer ratings, while Hong Kong and Istanbul had comparatively lower ratings.
Nearly 98.6% of customers leave only one review, showing limited repeat review behavior.
Over two-thirds of hosts are fully verified, improving platform trust and safety.
Listing growth peaked around 2015, followed by stabilization due to regulations and pandemic disruptions.
